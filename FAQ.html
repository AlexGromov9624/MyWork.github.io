WHAT IS TYPESONG?

TypeSong is an multinational song development company. As independent music continues to dominate the market so does the demand for content. TypeSong is comprised of Songwriters/Ghostwriters, Music Producers, and Recording Studio Performers. We have continued to bridge the gap between independent musicians, major record labels and sponsors.

WHO USES TYPESONG?

Our services are used by a wide range of musicians across multiple music genres, influencers wanting to capitalise their star power, record labels, and online media outlets. We have a wide range of services here

I WANT TO WORK FOR TYPESONG?

TWe are happy to hear from you kindly send us an email to jobs@typesong.com

DO YOU SHARE MY PERSONAL DETAILS?

We do not share your personal information such name, address, contact number with third parties. Your information is kept confidential and privately encrypted, strictly only project related information is used to achieve your job requirements.

CAN TYPESONG GET ME A RECORD DEAL?

TypeSong is not a record label and must remain impartial. However, depending on the service you have chosen such as a “Single deal”, negotiations with record labels will be made on request.

DOES TYPESONG TAKE A CUT OF MY ROYALITES?

You keep 100% of all royalties you earn from our service. No hidden fees, and depending on the package you choose, you may also be entitled to receive legal cover and shared royalty distribution.

WHAT IF I DON’T LIKE WHAT I RECEIVED?

In the unlikely event you are not happy with your project, we may offer several further revisions till you are absolutely satisfied.

HOW CAN I TRUST YOUR GHOSTWRITERS?

Our ghostwriting and ghost production services are sealed with compensation packages. Our authors sign confidently agreements before undertaking any work. If ever a contracted author breaches confidentiality, you may be immediately compensated up to £300 excluding any additional compensation you are owed.

I’M A REGULAR CUSTOMER CAN I GET SOME DISCOUNTS?

Yes, we have special offers and discounts up to 30%, please contact us or your project manager when you are ready!
